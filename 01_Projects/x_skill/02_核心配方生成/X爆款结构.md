## ðŸ“Š QUALITY CHECKLIST

Before you hit tweet, run these 6 tests:

1. **Stance Check:** Is there a clear POV? (If reader doesn't know what you believe, you failed)

2. **Proof Check:** Is there a specific number, name, or example? (Abstract = forgettable)

3. **Action Check:** Did you tell them what to DO? (Pure observation = engagement bait, not value)

4. **Confidence Check:** Did you delete "I think", "maybe", "in some cases"? (Hedge words kill authority)

5. **Rhythm Check:** Is every sentence short and punchy? (If a sentence is >25 words, split it)

6. **Screenshot Check:** Is there ONE line that can be screenshot and shared alone? (If not, rewrite the hook or CTA)
